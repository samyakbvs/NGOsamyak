Our Mission

On this website you can register to find out about NGOs and make contributions accordingly. We will provide a delivery facility to the contributors so that a direct delivery of their contributions can be made to the listed and chosen NGO. It’s a humble request that the contribution made should be consumable and portable, contributing any faulty items will not be appreciated. Also please contribute keeping the relevant units in mind for example, Rice & Wheat (kgs) and Oil (ltrs).

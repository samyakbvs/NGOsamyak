Uchit

The right choice

Uchit is a non-profitable trust pioneered by the youth to convert their aspiration: Altruism knows no boundaries, into reality. The trust aims to be a vehicle of dissemination. Uchit envisions a day when India thrives in parity and prosperity. Uchitâ€™s initial goal was to collect dry food, clothes, blankets, books, toys, materials that these underprivileged Indians are deprived of to let them know that we see them and we care. Now we aim to expand as a bridge between the NGO community and the contributors by providing a platform where we act as the carriers of happiness, we will collect the contributions and deliver them to the designated NGOs. Our vision is to see a world where maximum opportunities and resources are being sustainably utilized and that no area, no livelihood, no happiness is lost into oblivion.

On this website you can register to find out about NGOs and make contributions accordingly. We will provide a delivery facility to the contributors so that a direct delivery of their contributions can be made to the listed and chosen NGO. It's a humble request that the contribution made should be consumable and portable, contributing any faulty items will not be appreciated. Also please contribute keeping the relevant units in mind for example, Rice & Wheat (kgs) and Oil (ltrs).
